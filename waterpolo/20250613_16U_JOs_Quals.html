Tournament Format Summary

  • Group Stage: 16 teams divided into 4 groups play round-robin
  • Re-Bracket: Teams are re-seeded based on group placement:
    • AA Bracket: All 1st place teams compete for places 1-4
    • BB Bracket: All 2nd place teams compete for places 5-8
    • CC Bracket: All 3rd place teams compete for places 9-12
    • DD Bracket: All 4th place teams compete for places 13-16
  • Final Placement: Cross-bracket games determine final rankings
  • Venues: BBMAC (Friday), Granite Hills (Saturday-Sunday)
