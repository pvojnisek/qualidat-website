üèÜ About the Futures Water Polo League Super Finals

The Futures Water Polo League Super Finals represents the pinnacle of competitive youth water polo in the United States. Established to "provide high level competition for the top water polo clubs in the nation," this prestigious tournament brings together the most elite programs from across the country for an unparalleled championship experience.

The Futures WPL was created with a clear mission: to showcase, through its highly competitive environment, the collective talents and efforts of all those involved in youth water polo. All clubs, coaches, athletes, and officials are held to the highest standards to ensure a professional experience that mirrors elite collegiate and international competition.

üéØ Tournament Format & Structure

The 2025 Boys Division Super Finals featured 24 teams in the 16U Boys division, running June 27-29, 2025. Teams competed in regulation 25-meter pools with 7-minute quarters and 30-second shot clocks, maintaining the highest standards of competitive play.

The tournament format included comprehensive pool play (6 pools of 4 teams each) followed by elimination brackets, ensuring every team faced top-tier competition while competing for championship honors. Individual awards recognized outstanding performances, including MVP, Best Defender, Best Goalie, and Best Coach honors, along with All-Tournament Team selections.

üåü Championship Legacy

The Futures Super Finals has established itself as a premier showcase for college recruiting, with participating athletes going on to compete at top NCAA Division I programs. The tournament's reputation for excellence attracts scouts and coaches from the nation's most prestigious water polo programs.

The Bay Area venues provided world-class facilities that have hosted numerous national and international competitions, offering athletes an authentic championship environment that prepares them for the highest levels of competitive water polo.
