üèÜ About the 2025 Quiksilver International Water Polo Cup

The 2025 Quiksilver International Water Polo Cup represents one of the premier 16U boys water polo tournaments in Southern California. This prestigious event brings together 16 elite teams from across the region for an intensive 3-day championship format that ensures comprehensive competition and accurate final rankings.

The tournament features a sophisticated bracket system with pool play leading to dual elimination brackets, guaranteeing that each team plays exactly 5 games while competing for positions 1-16. The championship final at Long Beach City College provides a prestigious venue for the title match.

üéØ Tournament Format & Structure

The 16-team field is carefully seeded and organized into 4 pools of 4 teams each, with pool play determining advancement to either the Championship bracket (places 1-8) or Consolation bracket (places 9-16). The format ensures competitive balance while providing clear advancement paths for all skill levels.

All games feature 7-minute quarters with standard water polo rules, maintaining high-level competition standards throughout the tournament. The comprehensive schedule allows for proper rest between games while maximizing competitive opportunities.

üåü Championship Venues

The tournament utilizes multiple high-quality venues across Southern California, with the championship final taking place at the prestigious Long Beach City College. Additional venues include Buena Park HS, El Modena HS, and Golden West College, ensuring excellent facilities for all levels of competition.

The venue selection provides geographic accessibility while maintaining the prestige associated with championship-level water polo competition in Southern California.
